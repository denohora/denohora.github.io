Welcome!

I'm an experimental psychologist in the School of Psychology at the National University of Ireland in Galway.

In our lab, we investigate the dynamics of Learning and Applied Cognition. Our research uses fine-grained behavioral analyses to assess how the interaction between person and environment changes over time.

If you'd like to know more, click on the buttons at the top of the page to find out about the research we do, the people we work with here in NUI Galway, our published papers and our connections with labs across the world.
